I am human, like you.
Ordinary and unique.
Aware and blind.
Open and closed.
Loving and indifferent.
At peace and afraid.
Free and caged.
Perfect and flawed.
Warm and cold.
Righteous and hypocritical.
Accepting and judgemental.

I am a spectrum, complex and simple.
A paradox, really.
