I am human, like you.
Ordinary and unique.
Flawed and perfect.
Aware and blind.
Open and closed.
Loving and indifferent.
At peace and afraid.
Free and caged.
Warm and cold.
Righteous and hypocritical.
Accepting and Judgemental

I am a spectrum, complex and simple.
A paradox, really.
